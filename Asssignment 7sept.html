Lord Krishna

Heart of India

  • Lord Krishna is a major deity in Hinduism, known for his divine plays and teachings.
  • He is considered the eighth avatar or incarnation of Lord Vishnu.
  • Krishna was born in Mathura to Devaki and Vasudeva.
  • As a child, he performed many miracles and was known for his mischievous nature.
  • Krishna played a significant role in the Mahabharata war.
  • He delivered the Bhagavad Gita to Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.
  • Krishna is often depicted as playing the flute and surrounded by gopis (cowherd girls).
  • He is also known as the embodiment of love, and his love for Radha is celebrated in Hindu folklore.
  • Krishna is revered as a supreme God in many sects of Hinduism.
